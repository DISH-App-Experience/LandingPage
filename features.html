In Days.

At DISH, we can release your mobile application in days! We follow an efficient process which launches clients products super quick!

First, you must fill out our application form, click below or on any of the "Get Started" buttons on the website.

Second, we will privately notify you, and request some additional information.

Next, we send you email and password credentials to sign into DISH Connect, an app where you can manage your apps color theme, menu items, and use all of the features (and more!!) listed above!

Finally, you can customize and update your app via DISH Connect, as we prepare the final steps of releasing!

Busy Restaurant Owner? 
Clients are able to edit their mobile app on the go, after signing in with their specific credentials. If they are interested in DISH to set up their menu automatically, clients can pay an additional $250 for a launch fee and automatic menu integration.

All Features

Theme Customization

Make your app look and feel just like your restaurant! Add your logo, fonts and images in a matter of minutes.

Digital Menu

Manage and edit your menu in real time! DISH makes it easy for you to update your menu with little work. No need to contact developers to edit your menu, when you can do it yourself quickly!

Rewards Program

Do you have an existing punch-card / rewards program, or looking into implementing one? DISH's Rewards Program is simple and easy! Each users' punchard is on the app, for each franchised location.

Reservation Requests

Control your busy days with our reservation feature! Accept and deny user submitted requests from users for multiple locations. Reservations include start time, duration, number of seats, name, and more!

About Us Page

Create your custom About Us Page to inform your users about your story, and important contact details! You can even include your social media links to Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter!

Events & Promos

Alert your users whenever you have a special event or promotion. This can also be used to catch customers' attention via your new mobile app! It also drives more retention with special offers for mobile app users only!

Add Locations

Add your locations in order to secure different locations to scan your card at, give users directions to your nearest location, and assign custom promotions and events to certain locations.

Image Gallery

New restaurant, or looking to show off the interior of your location? Upload it into the Image Gallery, and keep your users updated on whats going on from the tips of their fingers.

Homegrown Analytics

Understand your customer demographics and app analytics to better know who and how to advertise to.

Marketing Options!

When checking out, choose from different marketing products for a low cost, set price! Choose from cards, to stickers, and more!
