About Us

DISH Connect is for restaurant owners who are interested in digitizing their business with mobile apps. With the DISH Connect App, they can get started in the digital restaurant business by managing their menu, implementing a rewards program, obtaining reservations, adding locations, promotions, and more. Using the mobile application strategy attracts a younger, more digital audience to their customer base, and increases business sales.

Driven By Our Own Values

We believe small businesses are some of the most interesting and meaningful places to assist, digitally. What they lack in size, they make up for with creativity, purpose.

Our Story

It all started by building static iOS applications for restaurants in and around Chicagoland, more specifically Elmhurst. After moving to Munich, Germany for two years, continuing to pursue the software business, and after receiving honest and proactive feedback, we worked and launched with European Restaurants. DISH then turned into a platform where you can (in real time) edit your own restaurant apps without any technical knowledge!

From All Over, Really!
DISH is developed world-wide. With developers and employees working from the United States, Mexico, Brazil, and Germany, we have been able to put together an incredible team to develop this platform; for dynamic restaurant mobile app covering 3 continents, Europe, North & South America.
