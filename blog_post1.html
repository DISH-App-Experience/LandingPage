About Chickies

Chickies Beef was started in 1962 on 28th and Pulaski by Bob and Madeline “Chickie” Bailey.  Everything at Chickies is homemade from our award winning Italian Beef, our famous hot peppers, fresh hand cut fries, to our made from scratch Italian Lemonade!  Our homemade Italian Beef has been voted “best beef in Chicago” and won many awards over the past 50 years. In 2007 Chickies was welcomed into the Vienna Hot Dog Hall of Fame.

The Chickies Beef mobile app is freshly designed with a up-to date menu, and access to in-app only promotions. Chickies also said that they were "thrilled" to take advantage of notifying their customers of special events at their location on Roosevelt Road.

The Mobile App

As Chickies has been getting great recognition with their Italian beef, they are on the way to becoming a well known, early built application on the iOS App Store, from their customer base. Users can explore and discover the inside of the restaurant, call the location from the app, access the menu, and get a peek at their booming social media. Best of all, app users have advantage of using in-app promos, a way Chuck, the store owner at Chickies, will communicate great deals and coupons with users! With soon to come rewards program and reservation system, Chickies is on the digital path, reaching a broader audience!
